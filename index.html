About Me

So there I was...minding my own business....

My name is Rob Warren and ALL good stories start out like that...I am a Web Developer who entered into the world of tech by a sequence of less than standard events. My path was winding and hilly, it turned back on itself several times but was always exciting, always moving.

After years of public service, nationally in the US Military and locally, as a Denver Police Officer, with the full support of my loving family, I decided it was time to reinvent my career and myself to focus on a job within Tech(my passion) that would allow me to be creative and experiment with all there is in the interwebs. So basically, I'm kind of a wizard. So if you are in need of some magic, give me a ring.
